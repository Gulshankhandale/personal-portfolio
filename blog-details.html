How to be appreciated for your hard work.

As a developer, you often need to choose what to optimise for, whether it's performance of the app, scalability of your infra or time to value metric. When it comes to getting recognised for our hard work, it's a similar problem. A while ago someone told me that in order to get noticed or appreciated, you need to make sure that your work is visible.

  • Figuring out what to optimise for
  • Be useful
  • Take responsibility for a high-impact project in your company
  • Make others more productive

Acknowledge accomplishments right away. The easiest way to recognize your employees for good work is to acknowledge their contributions right away.

Summery & Results

Adding more tests to increase the test coverage will always be appreciated. Bonus points if you're adding end-to-end tests covering core flows of your app; they are the most impactful in terms of value to the users (and by extension - your team), but they're also the most difficult to maintain. This kind of work shows that apart from shipping code, you also care about its quality.
